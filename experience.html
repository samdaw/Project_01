My experience

Pro:Direct

June 2015 – January 2016

Specialising in digital UI/UX design I created rich and engaging digital design solutions for one of the largest sports eCommerce sites and fledgling design agency. Achieved timely delivery of pixel perfect web designs for an international audience and continually demonstrated commercial awareness to elevate iconic sports brands such as Nike, Adidas and Puma.

UWE

January 2014 – June 2014

The publication 'We Live in Curious Times' was created by a small team of BA(Hons) Graphic Design students to promote the course and question the changing concept of the Graphic Design. It explored the role of the 'user' in modern society focusing on how information attracts and distracts vying for limited attention and was launched at the Arnolfini, Bristol.
